Tutorial!
How to upload a UX Mini Bootcamp certificate on LinkedIn
  1. Go to your LinkedIn profile
  2. Look for Akbar Wijaya's profile then Follow
  3. Scroll to the License and Certification section
  4. Click the + (add) icon
  5. Fill in all fields according to the required data.
  6. Then click the save button
Note:
The Credential ID is on your certificate and the Credential URL Column is filled with the following url address bootcamp.designjam.id/check/
